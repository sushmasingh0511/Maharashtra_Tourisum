Futala Lake

 

Futala Lake is one of the lakes in Nagpur in the Indian state of Maharashtra.The lake is spread over 60 acres.[1] Built by Bhosle kings of Nagpur, the lake is known for its coloured fountains. In the evenings the place is illuminated with halogen lights and Tanga (carriage) ride. The lake is surrounded on three sides by forest and a landscaped Chowpatty on one side.

Hedgewar Smriti Mandir

 

Dr. Hedgewar Smriti Mandir is situated in Reshimbagh, about 4kms south-east of Nagpur. It is a magnificent monument built in memory of Dr. Headgevar, a prominent personality of Nagpur city who was the founder of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS). Hegdewar smarak is a fine example of architectural skill that was visited by thousands of people every year.

RamDham

 

Ramdham, in a nutshell is Central India's largest cultural tourism park starring an amusement park, biggest OM of the world and, Shivlinga made of ice, likenesses of the famous temples of Maharasthra and India, including the Ashtavinayak, Dwadesh jyotirlinga and Vaishnodevi to name a few.

Ramtek Fort

 

Ramtekis a City and municipal council in Nagpur district of the Maharashtra, India.Ramtek has got Historic temple of lord Rama. It is believed that Ramtek was the place where Rama, the Hindu god, rested while he was in exile. Legend has it that the aashram of great Hindu sage Agastya was situated close to Ramtek.

Telankhedi Garden and Lake

 

Telankhedi Garden and Lake Telankadi Garden, located in the outskirts of Nagpur about 5kms from the city center besides Phutala Lake, is an ideal picnic spot with natural surroundings and lush green. This garden sprawls on 3.50 ha area was created by King Raghuji Bhosle (II) during 1780-1890. Telankadi Garden is excellent combination of Mughal garden architecture housing beautiful water pond and a marvelous aquarium with varoius range of fishes and fishing articles.

Navegaon National Park

 

Navegaon National Park is a national park located in the Gondia district of Maharashtra, India. Navegaon, a popular forest resort in the Vidarbha region, the easternmost part of Maharashtra, was built in the 18th century. The most unique experiences of the Navegaon National Park that you will have are going on a jungle safari, staying in a tree top house and riding a sail boat in the lake.
